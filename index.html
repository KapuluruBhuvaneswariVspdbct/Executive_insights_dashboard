📊 Executive Insights Dashboard

📈 Key Insights from the Sales Dashboard

  • 🔹 Top Performing Category: Electronics drives the highest revenue.
  • 🔹 Peak Sales Month: Sales peak in November due to seasonal promotions.
  • 🔹 High Revenue Region: The West Coast outperforms other regions.
  • 🔹 Customer Behavior: Repeat customers have higher average order values.
  • 🔹 Improvement Area: Office Supplies category underperforms across all regions.

🔍 Key Insights from the Customer Churn Dashboard

  • 🔸 Overall Churn Rate: Approximately 27% of customers churn annually.
  • 🔸 High Risk Segment: Customers with month-to-month contracts show the highest churn probability.
  • 🔸 Key Churn Indicators: High customer service calls and long resolution times are strongly linked to churn.
  • 🔸 Tenure Impact: Longer-tenured customers are less likely to churn, suggesting the importance of retention strategies early in the lifecycle.
  • 🔸 Payment Method Insight: Customers using electronic check are more likely to churn compared to those on credit card autopay.
