📊 Executive Insights Dashboard

📈 Key Insights from the Amazon Sales Dashboard

  • 🔹 Top Performing Category: Electronics consistently generates the highest revenue among all categories.
  • 🔹 Peak Sales Month: Sales sharply increase in November due to promotional events like Black Friday.
  • 🔹 Regional Performance: Western US states, particularly California, dominate in order volume and sales value.
  • 🔹 Customer Loyalty: Repeat customers exhibit higher average order values than new buyers.
  • 🔹 Opportunity for Growth: Office Supplies and Home Improvement categories show lower performance, suggesting potential for targeted campaigns.

🔍 Key Insights from the Customer Churn Dashboard

  • 🔸 Churn Rate: The overall customer churn rate stands around 27%, indicating room for retention improvement.
  • 🔸 High-Risk Profiles: Month-to-month contract holders are significantly more likely to churn compared to long-term subscribers.
  • 🔸 Churn Drivers: Increased customer service calls and unresolved issues are key churn predictors.
  • 🔸 Tenure Impact: Customers with longer tenure are less likely to leave, emphasizing the value of early engagement strategies.
  • 🔸 Payment Method Insight: Users paying via electronic check show higher churn rates than those on autopay systems.
